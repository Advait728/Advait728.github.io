Welcome to the world of piano!

Invented in the 1700s, the piano is an instrument of expression, creativity, and timeless beauty. Currently, over a quarter of familes own a piano - and this number continues to grow. The piano is far more than a mere instrument. As a core aspect of musical creation since its founding, the piano is a storyteller, a cultural barometer, a symbol of refinement and a lifelong teacher (pianorentals). The keys of a piano reveal a tapestry of our shared human history that is capable of acting as a bridge to various cultures, generations, and emotions. Whether you want to play piano, explore its history, or discover famous pianists, this site has something for you!



Significance

The piano has played a key role in various artistic periods including:

  • Medieval: - 1400
  • Renaissance: 1400 - 1600
  • Baroque: 1600 - 1750
  • Classical: 1750 - 1830
  • Romantic: 1830 - 1900
  • Modern/contemporary: 1900 - present
