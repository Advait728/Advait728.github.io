Learning Piano

Starting to learn the piano can be exciting! Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Start with learning the notes and their positions on the keyboard.
  • Practice scales and simple melodies to build your skills.
  • Focus on hand and finger positioning for better control and comfort.
  • Take regular lessons or use online resources to guide your progress.
  • Be patient, as learning an instrument takes time and practice.
